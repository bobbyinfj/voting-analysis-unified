What is King County Proposition 1?

King County Proposition 1, approved by voters on April 22, 2025, authorized a seven-year property tax levy beginning in 2026 to fund the Regional Automated Fingerprint Identification System (AFIS).

Purpose

Fund the Regional AFIS, a forensic tool used across all cities and unincorporated areas of King County to store and match fingerprint records for crime-solving purposes.

Tax Rate

The levy imposes a tax rate of 2.75 cents per $1,000 of assessed property value. For a median-priced home, this equates to approximately $15-20 per year.

Duration

Seven-year property tax levy beginning in 2026, running through 2032.

Election Result

Approved with 59.72% of voters in favor (151,495 votes in favor, 102,189 against).
