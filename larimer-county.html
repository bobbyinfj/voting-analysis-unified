Larimer County Elections Overview

Larimer County, Colorado, encompassing Fort Collins and surrounding communities, conducts regular elections for local offices and ballot measures affecting county services, transportation, and quality of life.

Location

Larimer County is located in Northern Colorado, home to Fort Collins, Loveland, and Estes Park. The county seat is Fort Collins.

Population

Approximately 359,000 residents, making it one of Colorado's fastest-growing counties.

Key Elections

County commissioner races, mayor of Fort Collins, city council seats, and various ballot measures on transportation, services, and infrastructure.

2025 Elections

Coordinated Election scheduled for November 4, 2025, with various ballot measures and local offices on the ballot.

  • Three commissioners elected county-wide
  • Staggered four-year terms
  • Oversee county services, budget, and policy
  • Represent both urban and rural interests
